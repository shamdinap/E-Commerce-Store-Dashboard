### üí° Key Insights and Recommendations

The analysis highlights critical areas for strategic focus:

#### 1. Repeat Visitor Value & Retention
* **Repeat Visitors** generate **19% more revenue per session** (\$4.74) than **New Visitors** (\$3.97)
* **Recommendation:** Prioritize maximizing retention efforts, such as email campaigns, loyalty programs, and site speed optimization, to leverage this higher customer value

#### 2. Marketing Campaign Performance (CVR)
*Highest Efficiency:** Brand campaigns (e.g., `bsearch - brand` with 0.089 CVR and `gsearch - brand` with 0.075 CVR) are the most successful in terms of traffic quality
* **Optimization Opportunity:** Non-Brand campaigns, specifically `gsearch - nonbrand` (0.067 CVR) and `socialbook - desktop` (0.052 CVR), represent the biggest lever for overall business growth, as they often attract new customers
#### 3. Top Seller & Refund Risk
* **Top Seller:** "The Original Mr. Fuzzy" is the highest revenue generator (\$1.21M) [cite: 124] [cite_start]and also the top seller by items sold[cite: 119].
  **Refund Concern:** "The Original Mr. Fuzzy" accounts for approximately **70% of all refunded money**, indicating potential quality or expectation issues that require immediate investigation despite its high sales volume
